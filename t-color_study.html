Color

Study

Spring 2020

Using over 200 scans of fabric that was purchased for thesis - scanning 1 to 8 physical layers at a time - this study of color and texture was conducted. The scans were digitally cut, layered, and inverted with some hints of solid color.

Scanned from polyester organza, polyester taffeta, cotton denim, lightweight plain-weave cotton, and fusible web.
