One side of working for Liora Manné is designing for wholesale import company, Trans-Ocean. They carry full lines of several different qualities, including (but not limited to) hand-tufted, hand-looped, and machine-made.

Trans-Ocean sells directly to customers, as well as to many retailers like Restoration Hardware and LLBean. A constant flow of new patterns is necessary to meet different buyers' requests, which allows Rebecca to explore many different fun concepts, themes, and motifs.
