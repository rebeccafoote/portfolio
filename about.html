Rebecca Foote is a South Korean-born, Italian-raised, Jersey girl (and current blonde) living in Brooklyn.

She is an alumna of Parsons School of Design where she majored in fashion design with a focus in materiality.

Applying the same outlook to design as she does to her hair color (refer to photo), Rebecca believes in having fun with her work. She has a deep love for the physical art of making and is often driven by technique and materials. Luckily for her, she finds meticulous craft can be a great vehicle for creative freedom.

She works now as a textile designer developing custom pieces for clients in residential and commercial interiors, as well as wholesale markets.
