Origami

Bags

Spring 2019

Chic, simple fabric handbags with minimal reinforcement stemming from a love of denim and heavy cottons.

Origami folds are used to structure one square or rectangular piece of fabric into a collapsible, 4-sided bag, thus producing very minimal fabric waste. Taking advantage of the created space the folds, they become accessible pockets for the user.

Made with 24oz 100% cotton canvas that has been synthetically indigo dyed. Each bag has a dyed canvas covered cardboard bottom insert.
